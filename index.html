The Science

Why Micro Dry Steam Is Different

Conventional steam cleaners contain up to 40% water. The Micro Cleaner's saturated micro dry steam contains only 5–8% water — producing ultra-fine steam molecules rather than wet droplets. This delivers superior cleaning power while leaving surfaces virtually dry.

At 180°C+ directly at the heating element, the steam kills bacteria, viruses, and multi-resistant germs through heat alone — no chemical assistance required.

Patented Stainless Steel Boiler

Tested to 70 bar with special welding. Built to outlast conventional boilers by years.

Self-Decalcifying Heating Element

Removable stainless steel element at 28W/cm² — four times the power density of standard heaters. Automatically prevents limescale build-up.

True Temp Technology

Electromechanical temperature probe maintains constant steam output throughout operation — no hot/cold fluctuations.

Continuous Operation

Two-chamber automatic refill means no cool-down waiting time. Designed for non-stop commercial shifts.
